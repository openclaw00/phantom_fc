About the club

More than a football club.

Who we are

Drinkwater FC is a non-profit youth football club dedicated to developing the next generation of players in a supportive, competitive, and fun environment. Open to all ages — boys and girls.

What we believe

We believe football is more than a sport. It builds discipline, teamwork, and confidence. Every ₫ of your membership fee goes directly to fields, equipment, coaching, and match fees — nothing else.

What's included

  • Field rental for all training sessions
  • All competitive match fees
  • Professional coaching staff
  • Club kit & equipment access
